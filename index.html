About Me

Throughout my entire life, I have had an avid interest in technology. As a child, I would take apart electrical components and try to understand how they work. Coding was a branch of technology that I became curious about after seeing it referenced in many movies in the 90's. 2020, has given me all the right components of time, resources, and motivation to take the leap into this new career path. I view software development as an opportunity to create, problem solve, and help others, all of which are passions of mine.

Prior to starting my coding journey, I had been working as a freelance worker for the entertainment industry for 9 years, working for major network shows, feature films, commercial advertisements, documentaries, reality TV, music videos, live performance production, and live broadcast production. This industry has taught me a lot about communication, problem solving, departmental budgeting, and personnel management.

Before working in film, I served in the U.S. Army for four years as a Special Operations helicopter Crew Chief at the rank of Sergeant.
